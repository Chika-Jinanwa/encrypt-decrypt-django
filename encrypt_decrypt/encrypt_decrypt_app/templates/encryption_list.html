Website Features

The website features the Encryption Engine, which currently supports the encryption techniques listed below. Click on the names of the encryptions to see detailed descriptions of the schemes and try to answer the challenge questions! The engine is case insensitive and produces all results in lowercase. If you don't specify a correct key, the engine will use the built-in default keys and will display these keys after it processes your request. If you decrypt a message and you're not getting what you want, check the key you provided and be sure it is accurate. The Engine is also a handy tool for players who battle to decrypt the Cipher Games . The Cipher Games is a list of encrypted messages which the players have to decode to earn points and get ranked on the leaderboard. Only the top 10 players are displayed on the board. Players progress from “Rookie Analyst” to the coveted title of “Cipher Lord” depending on the number of points accrued.
